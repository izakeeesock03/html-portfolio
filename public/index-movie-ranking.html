The Best Movies According to Izak

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Thor Ragnorok

I love the blend of comedic Thor and serious Thor. I also have always loved the Thor movies. From Asgard, to the imagery, the setting, to Thor the superhero himself. He's got cool powers.

Corpse Bride

Beautiful movie. Amazing songs. Simple plot, but rewatchable and sets the tone for the fall season.

Detatchment

Tear-jerking movie. Beautifully made, dealing with a serious issue that touches all of our hearts. My favorite serious-topic movie.
